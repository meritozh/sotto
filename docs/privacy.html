Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

The short version. Sotto has no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. Your subscription data stays on your devices and, if you choose, in your private iCloud. We never see it.

1. Who we are

Sotto is an independent application developed by an individual developer ("we", "our", or "the developer"). This policy describes how the Sotto application for iPhone, iPad, and Mac (the "App") handles information.

2. Data we do not collect

Sotto does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account credentials — the App has no sign-up or login
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports beyond Apple's standard, opt-in TestFlight and App Analytics frameworks managed by Apple
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) — Sotto contains no ads and no advertising SDKs
  • Device identifiers, location, contacts, photos, or microphone data
  • Any third-party tracking SDKs (no Facebook SDK, no Google Analytics, no Firebase, etc.)

3. Data you create stays on your device

Subscriptions, categories, payment methods, payment history, and your settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The developer has no access to this data.

4. Optional iCloud sync

If you are signed in to iCloud and have iCloud enabled for Sotto in your device settings, the App synchronizes your data across your devices using Apple's CloudKit private database. This means:

  • Your data is stored in your own private iCloud space, scoped to your Apple Account.
  • Only your devices, signed in to your Apple Account, can read it.
  • The developer has no ability to access, view, or export this data.
  • Apple's privacy policy and security practices apply. See apple.com/legal/privacy.

You can disable iCloud sync for Sotto at any time in Settings → [your name] → iCloud on iPhone or iPad, or System Settings → Apple Account → iCloud on Mac.

5. Exchange rates

To convert amounts between currencies, Sotto fetches daily exchange rates from Frankfurter, a free, open-source exchange-rate API published by the European Central Bank.

  • The request contains only the base currency code you have selected (for example, USD).
  • No subscription data, no personal information, no account information, and no device identifier is sent.
  • Frankfurter receives standard network metadata (your IP address and an HTTP user-agent), as with any web request. Sotto does not control or have access to Frankfurter's logs.
  • If you have no internet connection, Sotto uses the most recently fetched rates cached on your device.

6. App Store data

If you obtain Sotto from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect aggregate, anonymized analytics about downloads, installs, and crashes. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not by Sotto. The developer can see only aggregate, non-identifying statistics through App Store Connect.

7. Children's privacy

Sotto is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Because the App collects no personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from anyone, regardless of age.

8. Your rights

Because Sotto does not collect or store any of your data on our servers, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. You are in full control:

  • To delete your data on a single device: delete the App, or use the in-app reset.
  • To delete your synced data across all devices: sign in to iCloud.com or use Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and remove Sotto's data.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Material changes will be noted in the App's release notes.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email meritovibecoding@outlook.com or open an issue at github.com/meritozh/sotto/issues.
